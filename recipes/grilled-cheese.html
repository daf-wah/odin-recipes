Grilled Cheese

Making A Beautiful Lasagna

There is nothing quite like the warm and nostalgic memories evoked by a delicious grilled cheese sandwich. Memories of family dinners full of great aromas, loud laughter, and loving hugs. A timeless classic of a meal that never fails to top lists of favorites.

Continue reading below to discover how to bring about this mouth-watering meal for you and your loved ones:

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 3 tablespoons of butter, divided
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese

Making The Dish

  1. Preheat nonstick skillet on medium heat
  2. Butter one side of bread slice
  3. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet
  4. Add slice of cheese
  5. Butter a second slice and cover butter-side up
  6. Cook on both sides until lightly browned then serve
