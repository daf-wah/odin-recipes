Collard Greens

Making Glorious Collard Greens

There is nothing quite like the warm and nostalgic memories evoked by a delicious collard greens. Memories of family dinners full of great aromas, loud laughter, and loving hugs. A timeless classic of a meal that never fails to top lists of favorites.

Continue reading below to discover how to bring about this mouth-watering dish for you and your loved ones:

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3 slices bacon
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound fresh collard greens, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes

Making The Dish

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat oil in large pot over medium heat. Add bacon, cook until crisp, 5-7 minutes. Remove bacon from pan, crumble, and return to pan
  3. Add onion to pan and cook until tender, 5 minutes. Add garlic, cook until fragrant. Add collard greens and fry until wilting
  4. Pour in chicken broth and season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for approx. 45 minutes
