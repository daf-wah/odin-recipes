Making A Beautiful Lasagna

There is nothing quite like the warm and nostalgic memories evoked by a delicious lasagna. Memories of family dinners full of great aromas, loud laughter, and loving hugs. A timeless classic of a meal that never fails to top lists of favorites.

Continue reading below to discover how to bring about this mouth-watering dish for you and your loved ones:

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomato
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Eggs

Making The Dish

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let rest before eating
